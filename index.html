Fun Fact: How I Became a Cat Person 🐱

I used to think I was more of a dog person.

Cats always seemed a little too independent for me, like they had their own plans and were just letting humans exist nearby.

Then another one.

And another.

Before I knew it, our home slowly turned into a small cat household.

Over the past 4 years, I’ve cared for 15 cats in total. Seven of them are still with me today.

Safe to say… life at home is now run by cats.
